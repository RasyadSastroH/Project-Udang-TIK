Prediction Algorithm

  1. Priority 1: NH3 > 0.05 mg/L
    NH3 is the highest mortality risk factor
  2. Priority 2: DO < 4.0 mg/L
    DO is earliest mortality factor
  3. Priority 3: pH < 7 or pH > 8.5
    pH out of range causes mortality risk

NH3 (Ammonia)

Reason: Earliest predictor, increases toxicity exponentially

Impact: Gills damage, rapid death

DO (Dissolved Oxygen)

Reason: Low DO < 4 mg/L accelerates NH3 impact

Impact: Acute stress

pH (Acidity)

Reason: Controls NH3 conversion

Impact: High pH increases NH3 free form
